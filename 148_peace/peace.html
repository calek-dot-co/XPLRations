Peace

Zemo Magharo, Georgia

Situated on a hilltop plateau with sweeping views of south-eastern Georgia, this is the Peace monument by sculptor Nugzar Manjaparashvili.

Completed in the 1970s to overlook the valley below, its brutalism-inspired concrete form depicts flowing hair, draped fabric, and the levitating figure of a woman. Nearby, the local picnic area is shaded by 400 trees planted in memory of those lost during World War II.
