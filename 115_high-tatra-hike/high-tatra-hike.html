High Tatras Hike

Vysoké Tatry, Slovakia

The Carpathian mountain range spans eight countries, curving from Serbia north and then west to Austria, in a question-mark-like shape. The section I was hiking today was in the Western Carpathians, known locally in Slovakia as the High Tatras.

From my accomidation in Poprap on the valley floor, it was an easy 45 minute tram ride to Vysoké Tatry, where most of the hikes in this area can be started. As I arrvied at the base of the mountain it was pretty cloudly and much more snow in the lower areas than I had expected. This ruled out hiking to the higher Veľké Spišské lake, so instead I selected the lower Velické lake as today's destination.

As I traversed the side of the mountain range towards the lake, the clouds parted and the mountain came to life revealing the wonderful valley and mountain sights you see below.
