Soviet Viewing Platform

Sevan, Armenia

Located beside a highway in rural Armenia, this fish-tail spire was designed by Makabe Manuelian, a renowned Armenian architect celebrated for his Soviet-era modernist designs. Constructed in 1978 using stone, steel, and concrete, the structure is aptly named the Soviet Viewing Platform.

A narrow staircase leads visitors to the platform, offering sweeping views of the western shore of Lake Sevan, the town of Sevan, and the edge of the Dilijan National Park.
