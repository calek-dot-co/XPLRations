Kraftwerk Plessa

Plessa, Germany

Most of my abandoned exploring starts with a goal in mind. It’s either to reach a specific room, take a picture of something, or experience a piece of the past. My exploration in Plessa was no different. On the internet days before this visit, I saw a picture of this control room - without graffiti, without damage, as it was when it was in use. This was the goal.

The previous day I’ve been to Kraftwork Henningsdorf, and while it was great exploration, the control room has passed it prime – covered in graffiti and water damage. A fresh control room in great condition was something that I had to see.

The journey began with a three hour train ride, passed well trodden ground of Flugplatz Brand and into the unknown. On this Sunday morning, Plessa was quiet, with a few elderly neighbors out and about doing yardwork, but most of the town was still asleep. After a scouting lap of the block, I jumped the fence and climbed into the cooling towers.
