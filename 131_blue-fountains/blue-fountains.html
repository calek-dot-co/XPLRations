Blue Fountains

Gyumri, Armenia

On the opposite side of town from the better-known Iron Fountain lies Gyumri’s lesser-known counterpart: the Blue Fountains. This abandoned site features six blue-painted steel structures with a more decorative, ornamental design.

Also damaged in the 1988 earthquake, the fountain now gets very little attention, its frames repurposed as makeshift electrical poles for nearby homes.
