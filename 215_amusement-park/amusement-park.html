Abandoned Kids Park

Somewhere, Denmark

This children's wonderland has been closed for some time due to safety concerns. In the absence of maintenance, the attractions slowly fell into disrepair. A disappointing outcome given the unique character of this park. It had a bit of everything, from carriage rides to water slides, putting greens to pirate scenes.

Established in 1971, the play park is half of the site's footprint. The remaining half continues to operate as horse-riding centre with over 120 Icelandic horses. It was a quiet day on our visit, so even if we could be easily seen from the stables, there didn't seem to be anyone looking.

As we explored the small site, we found many other interesting things that would have once been a blast to play with as a kid. Two big water slides that exit the second floor of the building and abruptly end, leaving only concrete supports and the occasional pipe left over. Mini-golf with a volcano hole, maze hole, and geometric blockers. And finally, a small lake with a fake pirate ship complete with cannons and skeletons.
