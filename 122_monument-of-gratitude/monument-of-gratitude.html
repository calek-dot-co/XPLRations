Monument of Gratitude

Yerevan, Armenia

Crafted from tuff, this memorial features an eagle (symbolising the will and spirit of the people) and a pyramid (representing the renaissance of the Armenian people who survived). It expresses gratitude to the Arab nations and Syria for providing refuge and new beginnings to Armenians during the Armenian Genocide.

The monument stands as a testament to the enduring friendship between Armenians and Arabs, transcending borders and religious differences.
