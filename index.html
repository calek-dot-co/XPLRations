About xplr.es

The forgotten, the unknown, and the unusual have a magnet-like effect on me. I'm drawn in by the thrill of discovery, the wonder of exploring, and the impact of the experience.

XPLR is a catalog of my explorations from around Europe. Documented via photographs, often with writing, and occasionally with videos.

Cheers and enjoy exploring.
