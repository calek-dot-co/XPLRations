Dimitrios Shipwreck

Valtaki, Greece

Stranded on the southern coast of Greece, resting on the edge of the beach, lies the wreck of the cargo ship Dimitrios. Allegedly used for smuggling cigarettes between Turkey and Italy, the vessel was reportedly seized by port authorities in Gythio in the early '80s. The exact story of how it ended up beached 5km from the port remains unclear.

Some claim it was forced to leave after taking on water and being deemed unsafe, while others believe it broke free during a storm and was then deliberately set on fire to destroy evidence of smuggling.

Regardless of the reason for its arrival here on the beach, time is ensuring that the rusting wreck is slowly disappearing into the sand and sea.
