ROT-54

Orgov, Armenia

The Orgov Radio-Optical Telescope was proposed and developed on the side of Mount Aragats by Soviet-Armenian physicist and engineer Paris Herouni.

It operated for only three years until its closure in in 1990. Since then it has sat mostly abandoned, decaying from the weather and salvage...
