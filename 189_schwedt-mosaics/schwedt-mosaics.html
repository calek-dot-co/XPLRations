Mosaics from the GDR

Schwedt, Germany

Kosmonaut mosaic

Best known today for its crude oil and paper processing factories, the town of Schwedt on the Oder river also features several intriguing historical artworks. The first notable example is a vibrant mural of a cosmonaut reaching out into space.

By Erich Enge, 1967. Location: 53.058103, 14.293952

Friedrich Engels mosaic

Mounted on the side of an abandoned building is a 7.5-metre-high glass mosaic portrait of Friedrich Engels. The mosaic depicts the German philosopher and co-founder of Marxism gazing stoically into the distance, a common pose for propagandistic portrayals of intellectuals and Soviet-era cultural icons.

By Herdegen Fehlhaber & Werner Voigt, 1978. Location: 53.068540, 14.248736
