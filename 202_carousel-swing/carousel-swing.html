Fading Carousel Swing

Somewhere, Cyprus

This fading carousel swing, called ΑΦΡΟΔΙΤΗ (Aphrodite), was formerly a centrepiece of a Cypriot amusement park. Today, it sits abandoned amidst debris and overgrown grass. With its motor gone and gears seized by rust, the sun-bleached fiberglass panels only regain their glow with the help backlighting from the afternoon light.

A couple of year after first seeing photos of this location online, I finally made the journey to visit. Unluckily for this old carousel and me, those couple of years had been unkind. Once able to seat 24 people, the chairs and their chains have now almost all been stolen or destroyed.
