Twin Cooling Towers

Slovakia

Amid an ageing industrial park long past its prime, stand twin cooling towers. Modest by modern standards, yet they still tower above everything nearby, they sit abandoned. Unused and unappreciated.

Contact with rain colours the inside and out. Warm gradients of algae and concrete climb the outer walls from the base, while within, cool green hues spread from the centre, contrasting with the perimeter and interior walls.

Light pours in from overhead openings, while human-sized ovals invite access into the silent interiors. During my short visit, these towers were at least used for the first time in a long time.
