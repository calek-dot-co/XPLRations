Mausoleum to the Fallen Insurgents

Štulac, Serbia

This four-piece stone monument was built to memorialize the location of the first fight between the Yugoslavian Partisans and the German army in this area of Serbia. From the right perspective, this spomenik—crafted from gabbro stone—aligns to create a tunnel effect, potentially symbolizing the journey into the afterlife. It serves as a fitting memorial for the casualties of the Partisan resistance.
