Amtsgebäude R

Somewhere, Germany

This abandoned government office building is in a state of disrepair, with broken windows, graffiti, copper theft, and fire damage.

Yet, amidst the decay, a few well-preserved treasures remain: a painting depicting medics on a late 19th-century battlefield, a map of the German state Schleswig-Holstein, and a beautifully crafted attic framed by large wooden beams.
