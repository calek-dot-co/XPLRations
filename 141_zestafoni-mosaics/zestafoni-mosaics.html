Mosaic Walls

Zestafoni, Georgia

Entrance mosaic

Positioned along the main road qhen entering town from the west, this nine panel mosaic is located near the entrance to the Zestafoni Ferroalloy plant. Titled 'New Khalibs' by Demur Basheleishvili, it portrays workers transforming raw metals extracted from the Tkibuli and Chiatura mines into steel, something that has taken place non-stop in Zestafoni since the 1930s.

Location: 42.118276, 43.017500

Hotel mosaic

‘Land of Winegrowers and Metallurgists’ also by Basheleishvili is situated behind a barrier, presumably on private property, next to the plants former hotel building. I spoke with the security guard at a nearby gate, who kindly radioed for confirmation that I was permitted to visit.

Location: 42.117005, 43.028778

Missing mosaic

There was once a third mosaic titled 'Kapremont' located on the grounds of the Ferroalloy plant. Based on my research, it appears to have been demolished along with the abandoned building it was on. A photo of the mosaic, taken in 2013, is available via Dāvis Kļaviņš on Flickr.

Location: Unknown; likely demolished
