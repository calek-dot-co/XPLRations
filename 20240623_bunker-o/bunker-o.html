Bunker O

Bratislava, Slovakia

In a forest not so far from the center of Bratislava, numerous square pits scar the hillside, each with a ladder descending into the depths below.

Underground, a grid-like maze is concealed, a long-forgotten bunker that was once connected to a hospital now demolished.
