Pool Mosaics

Zestafoni, Georgia

Rising above each end of a long-dry pool are six mosaic panels in the town of Zestafoni. Each panel portrays a range of subjects—including mythical creatures, water sports, and a family—rendered in the visual language of socialist realism, with stylised, muscular figures in heroic stances.

Recently restored by the Ribirabo Foundation with support from the Polish Solidarity Fund PL in Georgia, these mosaics will soon be part of the Zestafoni New Industrial Route and Path of Mosaics.

To view the mosaics, speak with the guard at the booth to the right of the main entrance of David Abashidze Stadium. The guard appears to speak only Georgian and Russian, but a small entry fee (bribe) helps to open locked gates and doors.
