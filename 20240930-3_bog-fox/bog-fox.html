Soorebane High-Voltage Pylon

Rõuma, Estonia

The Estonian word Soorebane translates to 'bog fox', a quite literal description of the playful fox-like shape of Estonia's first designer powerline pole, which is conveniently located in a bog. Quite a cool sculptural shape to find on the side of the road on a drive towards the island of Hiiumaa.
