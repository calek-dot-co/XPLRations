NATO Radar Tower

Belgium

Since 1955, Air Force personnel have overseen the airspace above Belgium and Luxembourg with the help of a large radar installation, coordinating with two F-16 fighter jets stationed at a nearby airport for rapid interception.

Today, as operations shift to advanced, state-of-the-art facilities, much of the older equipment is left behind—abandoned, neglected, and open to exploration.
